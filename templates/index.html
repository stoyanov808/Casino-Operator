Info & Rules

40 Fixed Lines

Wins pay only left to right from reel 1. Minimum valid connection is 3 reels. Only connected winning symbols light up.

Payout-Based Rarity

Lower-paying symbols appear more often. Higher-paying symbols become harder to land on reels 3, 4, and 5.

Cluster-Style Drops

Fruits can still land in grouped areas, but cluster spreading is weaker after reel 2. Clusters do not pay by themselves.

Free Spins

3 Scatters award 10 Free Spins. 4 Scatters award 15. 5 or more Scatters award 20. During Free Spins, Wilds can show 2x or 3x multipliers.

Reveal Jackpot Bonus

After a paid base spin, there is a random chance to enter the reveal bonus. Reveal 3 matching Mini, Minor, Major, or Grand symbols to win that jackpot.
